Why Multiple Treatments Are Required

Multiple sessions are not a flaw in electrology. They are part of proper treatment design.

Hair grows in cycles, and only follicles with actively growing hairs can be effectively treated at a given appointment. Over time, repeat sessions allow your electrologist to target each follicle as it enters the right stage.

In practice, this means better long-term outcomes: consistent scheduling, systematic clearance, and measurable progress toward permanent reduction and eventual completion.
